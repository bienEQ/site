Bien ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and join our waitlist.

Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to:

We will not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your email address is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted database platform with industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure, access-controlled infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Retention

We retain your email address until:

  • You request its removal
  • You unsubscribe from our communications
  • We determine the information is no longer needed

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from our communications at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@bieneq.com.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies for advertising purposes. We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site.

Changes to This Policy
